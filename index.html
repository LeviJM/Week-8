About Us

The Joe Pesci Fan Club was founded on February 9, 1943 by the very nurses that helped deliver him.
They knew then and there, that this child would go on to release 3 studio albums, form a hit comedy duo, and star in at least 30 feature films, one of them being Home Alone 2: Lost in New York
We here at the Joe Pesci Fan Club abide by a simple code of conduct, reffered to as, The Way of Pesci.
We hope you enjoy your time here at the Joe Pesci Fan Club and emplore you to become a Member for a more in depth Pesci experience.
